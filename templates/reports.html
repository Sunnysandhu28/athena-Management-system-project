REG_14: Nutrition and Hydration

High Risk CQC

Root Causes:

  • Inadequate staff training on nutrition requirements
  • Poor documentation of dietary preferences
  • Inconsistent mealtime monitoring

Remediation: Implement standardized training and digital monitoring system

REG_16: Complaints Handling

High Risk CQC

Root Causes:

  • Staff lack training on complaint handling
  • Poor response time to complaints

Remediation: Deploy complaint management software and staff training
