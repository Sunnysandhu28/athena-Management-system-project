Category Information

The Cathena Policy Management System organizes policies into logical categories and subcategories for easy access. Each category contains policies related to a specific area of operation or compliance.

Key Categories:

  • Care Management - Policies related to care provision and management
  • Care Planning - Assessment, planning and implementation of personalized care
  • Data Protection - Guidelines for data privacy and compliance with regulations
  • Health & Safety - Policies to ensure workplace and client safety
  • Human Resources - Policies governing employment and staff management
