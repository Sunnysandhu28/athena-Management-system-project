Policy Replication Guidelines

Purpose: This feature allows you to manually view and replicate policy documents word-for-word into your own organization's format without directly downloading from QCS.
Step-by-Step Process
1
View the Original Policy

Select a policy from the list and click "View" to see the original PDF document. The system will display the content in a readable format.

2
Create a Template

Click the "Create Template" button to generate a Word document template with sections ready for you to fill in with the policy content.

3
Manual Replication

Using the template and viewing the original document, manually copy the content word-for-word while maintaining the document structure.

Best Practices for Replication:
  • Copy the exact wording from the original policy
  • Maintain the same section structure and hierarchy
  • Recreate tables faithfully with the same rows and columns
  • Include all lists and bullet points in the same order
  • Preserve any references to other policies or regulations
  • Match formatting styles for headings, emphasis, etc.
4
Upload the Replicated Document

When you have completed the replication, click "Upload Replicated Document" from the original policy view page to save your work to the system.

5
Compare the Documents

After uploading, you can use the comparison tool to verify that your replicated document accurately matches the original policy.

Quality Assurance
Accuracy Checklist
  • All sections are included
  • Text is copied verbatim
  • Tables are reproduced accurately
  • No content is omitted or altered
  • Document metadata (title, date) is correct
Common Issues to Avoid
  • Paraphrasing or rewording content
  • Skipping sections or paragraphs
  • Changing document structure
  • Altering policy references
  • Missing tables or visual elements
Technical Requirements
  • File Format: Only Microsoft Word (.docx) files are accepted for uploads
  • Maximum File Size: 10MB
  • Document Structure: Should include clear headings and sections
  • Styles: Use proper Word styles for headings (Heading 1, Heading 2, etc.)
Important: The policy replication process is designed for internal use only. Replicated policies should only be used within your organization according to your license agreement with QCS. Do not distribute or share replicated policies outside your organization.
