Working with Uploads

The Cathena Policy Management System uses machine learning to analyze policy documents. By uploading QCS policies, you help our system better understand policy structure and formatting.

  • Policy Uploads: Use the Policy Upload tool to submit QCS documents to improve our AI's understanding
  • Policy Simplification: Convert complex policies into simplified formats with our reverse engineering tool
  • File Formats: We support PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT, and Excel files
  • Categories: Organizing uploads by category helps our system learn policy context
