How It Works

Add Regulatory Requirements

Start by entering the regulatory requirements your organization needs to comply with. These could be from CQC, HSE, GDPR, or other regulatory frameworks.

Upload Supporting Documents

Upload evidence documents such as policies, procedures, inspection reports, or any other documentation that demonstrates compliance with requirements.

Enter Inspection Details

Record information about inspections, audits, or regulatory visits, including dates, inspector names, and preliminary findings.

Generate Comprehensive Report

Create a detailed compliance report that combines requirements, supporting documents, and inspection details with statistical analysis and heat maps.

Quick Start Guide

New to the system? Follow these steps for a complete compliance report:

  1. Add at least one requirement

    Enter the regulatory requirement details including framework, category and severity

  2. Upload supporting documents

    Attach evidence documents and link them to specific requirements

  3. Enter inspection information

    Record details about the inspection including dates and inspector names

  4. Generate your report

    Create a comprehensive report with heat maps and statistics
