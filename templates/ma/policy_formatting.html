Fire Safety and Emergency Procedures

Quenby Rest Home - Cathena Policy Management System

Registered Manager: Aj Sandhu

1. Purpose

This policy provides comprehensive guidance on fire safety and emergency procedures for Quenby Rest Home. All staff members must be familiar with these procedures to ensure the safety of residents and visitors.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all staff, contractors, visitors, and residents at Quenby Rest Home. The Registered Manager, Aj Sandhu, is responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy.

3. Key Responsibilities

  • All staff must complete fire safety training annually
  • Fire exits must be kept clear at all times
  • Fire drills must be conducted monthly
  • Fire equipment must be checked regularly
