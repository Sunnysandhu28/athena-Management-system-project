Regulatory Analysis System Demo

About This Demo

This is a demonstration of the Regulatory Analysis System that showcases the key features and functionality. In a fully implemented version, this system would:

Document Analysis

Upload and analyze documents against multiple regulatory frameworks with automatic scoring and categorization.

Compliance Tracking

Track compliance across multiple regulatory frameworks with visual heat maps and progress indicators.

Action Planning

Generate and manage action plans with priorities, assignments, and due dates based on compliance findings.

Inspection Management

Schedule, prepare for, and document regulatory inspections with comprehensive tracking and reporting.

Evidence Management

Organize and manage compliance evidence with categorization, tagging, and linkage to regulatory requirements.

Comprehensive Reporting

Generate detailed reports on compliance status, actions, and trends with customizable formats and filters.

Integration with Policy Management

This system is designed to integrate with your Policy Management features, creating a comprehensive solution for regulatory compliance and policy management.
