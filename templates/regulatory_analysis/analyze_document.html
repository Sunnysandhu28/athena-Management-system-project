About Regulatory Analysis

This tool analyzes documents against multiple regulatory frameworks:

  • CQC Regulations - For healthcare and care service compliance
  • RIDDOR - For workplace incidents and injuries
  • Fire Safety - For fire prevention and safety requirements
  • Financial - For financial and accounting regulations
  • Business Governance - For corporate governance and compliance

The analysis will identify:

  • Matched regulations and requirements
  • Key deadlines and responsibilities
  • Common patterns and best practices
  • Heat maps of regulatory focus areas
Document Examples

Suitable documents for analysis include:

  • CQC inspection reports
  • Internal audit documents
  • Policy and procedure documents
  • Risk assessments
  • Incident reports
  • Corporate reports and whitepapers
