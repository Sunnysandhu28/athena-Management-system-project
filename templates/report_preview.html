CQC Warnings Analysis

CQC Warning 1: Staffing Issues

Analysis of staffing data identified significant concerns with staffing levels, skill mix, and deployment. Key issues include:

  • Insufficient staffing during peak periods (20% below requirements)
  • Inadequate skill mix on night shifts (only 65% with dementia qualification)
  • Poor staff deployment during mealtimes
  • High agency usage (28%)
  • Insufficient supernumerary leadership coverage
