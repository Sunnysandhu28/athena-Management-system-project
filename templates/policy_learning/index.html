What is AI Policy Learning?

AI Policy Learning is a feature that allows the system to analyze policies and learn from their structure and content. This helps to:

  • Extract metadata from policies to make them more searchable
  • Identify common patterns in policy structures
  • Generate missing sections based on policy context
  • Improve the quality and consistency of policies
